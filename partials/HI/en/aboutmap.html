About this Map. This map depicts the tsunami evacuation zones (shaded in pink) developed by your local emergency management or county civil defense agency. These evacuation zones provide the minimum safe evacuation distance.

This information is also available in your local telephone directory and from your county civil defense agency.

In the event of a locally generated tsunami, you may only have minutes to evacuate. If the ground is shaking, a locally generated tsunami may be occurring. Move to higher ground immediately, well away from the evacuation zone and low-lying coastal areas.
