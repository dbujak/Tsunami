About this Map

These maps depict the tusnami evacuation zones (shaded in pink) developed by your county civil defense agency. These evacuation zones provide the minimu safe evacuation distance.

These are the same zones that are published in the Hawaiian Telecom Yellow Pages and available from your local civil defense agency.

In the event of a locally generated tsunami, you may only have minutes to evacuate! If you feel the ground shaking, a locally generated tsunami may be occurring. Move to higher ground immediately, well away from the evacuation zone and low-lying coastal areas.

Kaua'i Civil Defense asks that where feasible, persons leaving the evacuation areas should proceed to at least the 100' elevation contour/level shown on the map in red, so as not to cause traffic congestion in or near evacuation zones.
