Tsunami Evacuation

If you are in a low lying area near the coast or in the tsunami evacuation zone and you feel shaking that causes you to fall or have difficulty standing, get to higher ground immediately, a locally generated tsunami could reach the shore within minutes!

Evacuation Tips

When Sirens Sound

Follow the evauation order issued by authorities.

If you are in the evacuation zone, prepare to move inland. If located outside a tsunami evacuation zone, stay outside of the evacuatio zone and limi all non-emergency travel.

For strong local earthquakes: If you have access to an undamaged concrete or stell reinforced building of 6 or more stories, move above the third floor and stay put.

For distance source tsuanmis, you may have more time to react. A Tsunami Warining will be issued three hours prior to the estimated arrival time. If you are in the evacuation zone when the warning sirenes sound, prepare to move inland, or If you have access to an undamaged concrete or stell reinforced building of 6 or more stories, move above the third floor and stay put.

Avoid traffic gridlock! It may be safer and more efficient to walk out of the tsunami evacuation zone.

Remain outside the evacuation zone until the All Clear signal is issued by local civil defense authorities.

Do not use your telephone or cell phone except for emergencies.

Save yourself, not your possessions.

Remember, all public schools in tsunami evacuation zones have emergency evaucation plans in the event of a Tsunami Warning.
